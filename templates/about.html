About Us

About

At City Discoverer EasyDesk, we believe work should be flexible, affordable, and inspiring. Located at 50 Stately St, Suite 2, Wiley Ford WV 26767—just minutes from downtown Cumberland, MD—we provide a professional coworking space where individuals, freelancers, and small teams can thrive.

Our space is designed for productivity, connection, and discovery. Whether you need a quiet desk for a few hours, a weekly pass for consistent work, or a monthly plan for your business, EasyDesk adapts to your needs.

Services

  • 📅 Hourly Desks – book a desk by the hour and pay only for the time you need.
  • 🌅 Day Passes ($25/day) – enjoy unlimited access to our desks for a full workday.
  • 📅 Weekly Passes ($100/week) – flexible access for 7 days of productivity.
  • 📆 Monthly Passes ($300/month) – your dedicated coworking base for 30 days.
  • 🪑 Meeting Lounge – a comfortable space for small groups or overflow seating when desks are full.
  • 🌐 Amenities – strong Wi-Fi, office seating, and a professional environment to help you focus.
