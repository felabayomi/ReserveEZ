How It Works

For Guests (Customers)

1

Find a Restaurant

Browse participating restaurants in your area. Filter by cuisine type to find exactly what you're craving.

2

Select Date & Time

Choose your preferred date, time, and party size. See real-time availability so you know exactly what's open.

3

Confirm Reservation

Some restaurants may require a small deposit to secure your booking. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

4

Receive Confirmation

You'll receive an email confirmation with your reservation details, plus reminder notifications before your reservation.

5

Arrive & Enjoy

Arrive at your scheduled time and enjoy your dining experience. No more long waits!

Need to Cancel or Reschedule?

You can manage your reservation using the link in your confirmation email. Cancellation and rescheduling are subject to the restaurant's cancellation policy.
