How It Works

For Restaurants

1

Create an Account

Register your restaurant through the ReserveEZ Restaurant Portal. Provide your restaurant details, cuisine type, and contact information.

2

Configure Settings

Set up your business hours, reservation availability, deposit requirements, and cancellation rules — all customizable to fit your restaurant's needs.

3

Add Tables

Customize your table layout with table names, seating capacity, and table types (booth, patio, window, counter, private dining, and more).

4

Connect Stripe

Securely connect your Stripe account to receive deposits directly into your bank account. Setup takes just a few minutes.

5

Start Accepting Reservations

Guests can now book online 24/7. You control availability, reservation approvals, policies, and payment requirements.

You're in Full Control
  • Set your own deposit amounts and cancellation policies
  • View and manage all reservations from your dashboard
  • Track no-shows and earnings in real time
  • Receive instant email notifications for new bookings and cancellations
