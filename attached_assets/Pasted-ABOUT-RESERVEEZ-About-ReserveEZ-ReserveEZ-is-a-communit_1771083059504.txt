ABOUT RESERVEEZ

About ReserveEZ

ReserveEZ is a community-focused online reservation platform designed to help restaurants manage bookings efficiently while improving the dining experience for guests.

We created ReserveEZ to solve a common challenge in growing communities: long wait times, overcrowding during peak hours, and the lack of structured reservations for special occasions.

Our mission is simple:
	•	Help restaurants increase predictable revenue
	•	Reduce no-shows and operational stress
	•	Improve guest satisfaction
	•	Make dining more organized and enjoyable

ReserveEZ provides a secure, easy-to-use system that allows guests to reserve tables online while giving restaurants full control over availability, deposits, and table management.

We believe technology should simplify operations — not complicate them.

HOW TO USE RESERVEEZ

For Guests (Customers)

1. Find a Restaurant

Browse participating restaurants in your area.

2. Select Date & Time

Choose your preferred date, time, and party size.

3. Confirm Reservation

Some restaurants may require a small deposit to secure your booking.

4. Receive Confirmation

You’ll receive:
	•	Email confirmation
	•	Reminder notifications before your reservation

5. Arrive & Enjoy

Arrive at your scheduled time and enjoy your dining experience.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, you can do so according to the restaurant’s cancellation policy.

⸻

For Restaurants

1. Create an Account

Register your restaurant through the ReserveEZ Restaurant Portal.

2. Configure Settings

Set:
	•	Business hours
	•	Reservation availability
	•	Deposit requirements
	•	Cancellation rules

3. Add Tables

Customize your layout:
	•	Table names
	•	Seating capacity
	•	Table types (booth, patio, etc.)

4. Connect Stripe

Securely connect your Stripe account to receive deposits directly into your bank account.

5. Start Accepting Reservations

Guests can now book online 24/7.

You control:
	•	Availability
	•	Reservation approvals
	•	Policies
	•	Payment requirements
